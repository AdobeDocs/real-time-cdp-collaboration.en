# Configure administrator access for Collaboration Starter onboarding

As an invited Starter user, you must grant yourself product administrator privileges to access Collaboration. This guide explains how to set up administrator access in the Admin Console so you can configure permissions for Collaboration and perform essential functions such as managing user access to the product.

## Prerequistes {#prerequisites}

Before continuing, ensure that you have:

* Accepted the invitation from your licensed Collaboration partner. For more information about the invitation requirements, see [Collaboration Starter overview](../overview/starter-overview.md#prerequisites).
* Reviewed and signed the Collaboration Starter terms and conditions.
* Received your Adobe welcome email and completed your first-time account creation.

## Set up admin access {#setup-admin-access}

When your Adobe account is created, you are assigned the system administrator role. As a system administrator, you can manage your organization's users, permissions, and product access within the Admin Console. Use the Admin Console to grant yourself user access to the Experience Platform products. You also need to add yourself as a product administrator to perform administrative tasks within a product such as managing user access to Collaboration.

To learn more about roles and products in Experience Cloud, read the [access control overview](../permissions/overview.md) documentation. 

### Configure product administrator access (optional) {#configure-product-admin-access}

To begin, sign in to [Adobe Experience Cloud](https://experience.adobe.com/){target="_blank"} with your credentials. You can see a list of your available products within the **[!UICONTROL Quick access]** section. Select **[!UICONTROL Admin Console]**.

<!-- ![Adobe Experience Cloud homepage with Admin Console highlighted.](../../assets/setup/starter/select-admin-console.png){zoomable="yes"} -->

The [Admin Console](https://adminconsole.adobe.com/) workspace opens in a new tab. Select **[!UICONTROL Adobe Experience Platform]** from the **[!UICONTROL Products]** list under **[!UICONTROL Products and services]**.

<!-- ![Admin Console workspace with the the Adobe Experience Platform product highlighted.](../../assets/setup/starter/admin-console-workspace.png){zoomable="yes"} -->

In the **[!UICONTROL Adobe Experience Platform]** product dashboard, navigate to the **[!UICONTROL Admins]** tab, then select **[!UICONTROL Add admin]**.

<!-- ![Adobe Experience Platform product dashboard with the Admins tab and the Add admin option highlighted.](../../assets/setup/starter/add-admin.png){zoomable="yes"} -->

Enter your email address or username in the **[!UICONTROL Add product administrators]** dialog, then select the correct account from the dropdown. Once finished, select **[!UICONTROL Save]**.

<!-- ![The Add product administrators dialog displays your account's information and the Save option selected.](../../assets/setup/starter/add-product-administrators.png){zoomable="yes"} -->

Confirm that you are added as a product administrator of the **[!UICONTROL Adobe Experience Platform]** product.
